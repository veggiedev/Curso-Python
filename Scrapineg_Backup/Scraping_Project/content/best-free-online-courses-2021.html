Class Central’s Best Online Courses of the Year (2021 Edition)

Class Central’s list of the highest-rated free online courses of the year

Heba Ledwon Dec 27th, 2020
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Envelope
  • 2

As of each year, Class Central has tallied the best courses of the current year, based on thousands of learner reviews. Here are the rankings from 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, and 2019.

Of all the learners that ever registered on a MOOC platform, one third did so in 2020. Class Central itself received 18M learners and 70,000+ reviews in 2020.

This is why we are calling 2020 the Second Year of the MOOC.

Now in its ninth year, the modern MOOC movement has crossed 180 million learners. Class Central data shows that providers launched over 2,800 courses, 360 microcredentials, and 19 online degrees.

Methodology

The new courses below are the thirty most highly ranked on Class Central. We made a list of all the courses that were released in 2020. The 2,800 courses were then ranked based on the Bayesian average of the thousands of reviews that came in during the year.
